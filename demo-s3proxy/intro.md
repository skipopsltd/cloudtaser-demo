# CloudTaser S3 Proxy

Client-side encryption for object storage. Data is encrypted before it leaves your application — the cloud provider stores only ciphertext. Encryption keys stay in EU/UK-hosted Vault, never shared with the provider.

## ⚠️ Before you click Start

The environment takes **1–2 minutes** to prepare after you press Start. A setup script installs K3s, deploys the S3 proxy, and configures the EU vault.

**Look at the terminal on the right** — wait until you see the interactive demo menu before proceeding. Do not click through the steps in this panel until the terminal is ready.
